For

The Legend

Mahendra Singh Dhoni

Most of us know about the achievements of Dhoni Sir. But here are some less known facts about the legend.

Less known facts about "

Captain Cool

"

➤ Football and Badminton Enthusiast: Before pursuing cricket, Dhoni was an avid football player and was even selected to play at the district and club level. He also excelled in badminton and was a goalkeeper for his school's team.

➤ Ticket Collector at Indian Railways: Dhoni worked as a ticket collector at the Kharagpur Railway Station in West Bengal during his early cricketing days. Despite the challenges of balancing work and cricket, he continued to pursue his passion for the game.

➤ Helicopter Shot Originator: Dhoni is credited with popularizing the "Helicopter Shot" in cricket. While he didn't invent the shot, he perfected and popularized it, making it one of his signature strokes.

➤ Passion for Bikes: Dhoni is a passionate bike enthusiast. He owns a vast collection of motorcycles, including high-end models like the Kawasaki Ninja H2, Confederate Hellcat, and Yamaha Thundercat.

➤ Honorary Lieutenant Colonel: Dhoni holds the honorary rank of Lieutenant Colonel in the Indian Territorial Army. He underwent training with the Parachute Regiment in 2015 and has participated in various army-related activities.

➤ Ranchi's First Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Awardee: Dhoni became the first recipient of the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award from his hometown, Ranchi, Jharkhand. He received this prestigious award in 2007 for his outstanding achievements in cricket.

➤ Charity Work: Dhoni is actively involved in charitable initiatives. He established the "Mahendra Singh Dhoni Charitable Foundation" to provide education and healthcare support to underprivileged children.

➤ Passion for Military and Uniforms: Dhoni has a deep admiration for the military and has been seen sporting various military-themed tattoos and accessories. He is also known for his love of camouflage-patterned clothing.

➤ Skipper in All Three Formats: Dhoni is the only captain in cricket history to have led his team to victory in all three major ICC tournaments – the ICC T20 World Cup, ICC Champions Trophy, and ICC Cricket World Cup.

➤ Humble Background: Dhoni hails from a humble background. His father, Pan Singh, worked in junior management at MECON (Metallurgical and Engineering Consultants) while his mother, Devaki Devi, is a housewife. Dhoni's rise to cricketing stardom is a testament to his hard work and determination.

➤ MS Dhoni is also known as “Captain Cool”. The simple reason behind this nickname is his calm attitude. His cool and calm temperament has won India a number of matches. Dhoni never lost his coolness in any situation, whether he was winning or losing, he remained calm.
